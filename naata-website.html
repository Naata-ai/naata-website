How It Works
Three Spaces, One Bond
तीन जगहें, एक नाता
naata.ai gives each partner their own private AI wellness space — and a joint space where the AI bridges both perspectives without revealing private conversations.
💭
Your Private Space
मेरी जगह
A safe, private space where you talk to the AI about your feelings, frustrations, and hopes. Your partner never sees this. Ever.
🔒 Completely Private
💭
Partner's Private Space
उनकी जगह
Your partner has their own equally private space. The AI understands both of you — independently, deeply, without judgement.
🔒 Equally Private
🌉
The Bridge
दो दिलों का पुल
A joint session where the AI mediates using understanding from both private spaces — without ever revealing what either partner shared privately.
✨ AI-Mediated

Speak naturally — the AI listens through your microphone, converts your voice to text, and responds. No typing needed. Just talk like you would to a friend.

Our Story
We Built This for Us First
पहले अपने लिए बनाया, फिर आपके लिए
We're Nivedita and Vishal — married, building a life together, figuring it out one day at a time. Like most Indian couples, we don't fight about the big stuff. It's the small things — the tone, the silence after a long day, the things you want to say but don't because "log kya kahenge."
We looked for help. Professional support costs ₹3,000 a session. Apps built in the US don't understand joint families, in-laws, or why "adjustment" is a loaded word. Nothing felt like it was made for couples like us.
So we built it. naata.ai is the companion we wished we had — something that listens without judging, understands our culture, and is there at 2 AM when you can't sleep because of an unresolved argument. No appointments. No stigma. Just a safe space to talk, reflect, and grow closer.
— Nivedita & Vishal
Nivedita Lunia, Co-Founder & CEO  ·  Vishal Lunia, Co-Founder & Chief AI Strategist
